Multi‑Agent Research → Verify → Brief → Act
Objective: Formalize agent roles as a state machine/graph with policies, memory, and human‑in‑the‑loop approvals.

Agents

Planner: decomposes task, sets tool policies.

Researcher: queries retrieval/search, browses sources.

Fact‑Checker/Critic: cross‑source agreement; defamation/red flag checks.

Briefing Writer: composes cited summary.

Executor: creates alerts, tickets, influencer lists; waits for approval.
